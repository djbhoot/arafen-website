How to use Ad Buddy

Use this calculator before launching or scaling a paid acquisition campaign. It helps you understand whether your CPC, conversion rate, close rate, deal value, and gross margin can support profitable growth.

  • ROI shows estimated campaign profitability.
  • ROAS shows revenue efficiency compared to ad spend.
  • Break-even CPC shows the maximum cost per click you can afford.
  • CPA estimates your paid acquisition cost per customer.
