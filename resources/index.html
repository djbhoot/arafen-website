How to use this ad ROI calculator

Use this calculator before launching Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Meta Ads, or any paid acquisition campaign. The goal is not to predict the future perfectly. The goal is to see whether your numbers are even close to workable.

  • ROI% shows how much gross profit you generate for every dollar spent.
  • Break-even CPC shows the maximum cost per click you can afford before the campaign loses money.
  • CPA shows estimated customer acquisition cost from paid ads.
  • Scenario profit helps you compare conservative, expected, and aggressive conversion assumptions.
