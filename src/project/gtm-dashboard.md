# AI-Powered GTM Dashboard

A product-style sample project for early B2B SaaS teams. It shows how leads, scoring, AI outreach, campaigns, inbox signals, calendar activity, and GTM metrics can live inside one calm operating workflow.

[Open the interactive dashboard demo](/projects/)

## The problem

Early teams often run GTM across spreadsheets, inboxes, AI tools, CRMs, and calendars. The result is scattered execution, weak prioritization, and limited feedback loops.

## The strategy

I designed the dashboard around one simple GTM sequence:

- Import leads
- Score accounts
- Generate outreach
- Track replies
- Book meetings
- Review pipeline metrics

The goal was not to create another complex CRM. The goal was to create a simple operating layer for founder-led and lean growth teams.

## What the dashboard includes

- Lead pipeline with account score, country, company size, pain point, and status
- AI outreach generator based on market, segment, pain point, and tone
- Campaign cards by country and audience segment
- Reply-rate and company-size performance views
- Inbox signal examples for demo intent, buying questions, and nurture opportunities
- Calendar view for demos, follow-ups, and no-show risk
- Setup roadmap for turning the static prototype into a live product

## Expected impact

This project demonstrates GTM system thinking, product workflow design, AI-assisted outreach, and the ability to convert messy marketing operations into a usable dashboard.

For hiring managers and founders, it shows that I can connect strategy with execution: not just write campaigns, but design the workflow behind them.
